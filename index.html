NBA Postseason Probability Model (last update: 01/28/2019)

These predictions were generated using an ensemble of 10,000 RandomizedSearchCV tuned and 5-fold cross-validated XGBoost classifier models using the neg_log_loss scoring argument built from win percentage, mean age, and offensive and defensive per game team statistics from the 1973-1974 ABA season season until the 2017-2018 NBA season (1103 teams in total) provided by basketball-reference.com. After the 10,000 models were fit on the training data, predicted probabilites on the test data were averaged to construct a confusion matrix and compute/visualize the ROC AUC. Simultaneously, after each of the 10,000 models had been fit, predictions on the current data were generated and averaged. The code for this model can be found here
